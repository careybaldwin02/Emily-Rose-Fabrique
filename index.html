    Features and services

    We view each project as unique and we work hard to align our approaches with the specific needs of our clients. Whether you are a designer in need of costumes for a production or a client looking to upgrade your wardrobe with a few quality custom items, you can expect our undevided attention throughout the planning and construction process.

    • Consultation and Measurements

      We take the time to understand every aspect of your project. We offer a flexible schedule of appointments and we are able to travel to you, both locally and beyond depending on the size and scope of your project. Once we decide to move forward, we will gather measurements and offer choices for materials.

    • Collaboration and Refinement

      Our unique process involves continuous correspondence throughout the construction process. If the fit isn't just right, we will make alterations until we achieve your ultimate satisfaction.

    • Final Product

      See your design come to life knowing that every detail has been handled with the utmost care. Add that perfect piece to your everyday attire or dress for that special event in an outfit made just for you: perfect color, perfect material, perfect fit.
